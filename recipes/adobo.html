Adobo

Description

A Filipino stew made with pork belly. The sauce is made with a tomato, soy sauce and vinegar base that makes it slightly tart. If you're unfamiliar with Filipino cuisine, this classic dish is perfect for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Pork belly
  • 1 bulb Garlic
  • 1 large Onion
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup Vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 3 Bay leaves
  • 1 TBSP Sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Brown garlic in oil at medium-low heat and set aside
  2. Saute onions
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until pulverized
  4. Add garlic and pork belly
  5. Add soy sauce, vinegar, water, salt and pepper
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for about 2 hours
