Rory's Mac & Cheese

Description

A copycat recipe of Chick-Fil-A's gooey, creamy, crispy mac and cheese. This recipe is easy to make and sure to keep your kids(and adults) happy!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Elbow macaroni
  • 2 TBSP Butter
  • 2 TBSP All purpose flour
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 2 cups Whole milk
  • 1 Egg yolk
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 4 oz Extra sharp cheddar cheese(separated)
  • 3 TBSP Italian blend cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and add macaroni
  2. Melt butter on medium low heat
  3. Add butter and cook until smooth
  4. Add milk and cook until no clumps remain
  5. In a separate bowl, add yolk and spoonful of hot milk mixture and whisk
  6. Add yolk mixture to pan
  7. Add cheeses and cook until melted
  8. Add spices
  9. Add macaroni
  10. Place everything in baking pan
  11. Top with remaining cheddar cheese
  12. Place in oven and broil until cheese is golden brown
